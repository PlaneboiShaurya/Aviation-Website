Indian Air Force

About

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is the aerial branch of the Indian Armed Forces. It plays a crucial role in safeguarding India's airspace and conducting aerial operations. With a rich history and modern capabilities, the IAF is integral to India's national security. The Indian Air Force has played crucial role in all the wars which our country has fought. The Indian Air Force also contributes immensely in nation building and actively participates in Disaster Rescue missions. It supports Army and Navy in their land and maritime operations. The Indian Air Force boasts the largest female workforce among all the 3 Armed Forces.

Eligibility Conditions

  • Age: Candidates must not be older than 23.
  • Education:Minimum 10+2 qualification with Physics and Mathematics and must hold a graduation degree from a recognized university.
  • Nationality: Must be a citizen of India.
  • Physical Standards: As per Indian Air Force standards.
  • Medical Fitness:Candidates must be medically fit as per Air Force standards.

Selection Procedure

  • A written exam conducted by the UPSC.
  • The exam is followed by an SSB (Services Selection Board) Interview.
  • The selected candidates undergo a full-body medical check-up.
  • The merit list is prepared based on written and SSB marks.
  • Joining letters are issued based on the vacancies and merit.
  • Extra test CPSS(Computerised Pilot Selection System Test) is conducted.
  • The Medical exam of the Indian Air Force is the most rigorous and stringent.

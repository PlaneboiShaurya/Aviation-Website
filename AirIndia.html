Air India

About

Established in 1932 as Tata Airlines, later nationalized in 1953, Air India has been a prominent figure in India's aviation industry, offering domestic and international flights. Despite financial and operational hurdles, it remains a key player. Recently it was privatized and sold back to TATA signaling a commitment to competitiveness and improved services amidst the dynamic global aviation landscape. Recently Air India has ordered 470 Aircrafts both Wide Body and Medium Body Aircrafts. It has also announced a merger with Vistara and Air Asia thereby increasing its fleet size. Air India would require 1000+ Pilots in future so there's a huge scope of jobs in this Airline.

Eligibility Conditions

  • Age: Candidate must be older than 18 years.
  • Education: Minimum educational qualifications required Class XIIth with Physics and Mathematics.
  • Nationality: Must be a citizen of India.
  • Physical Standards: As per DGCA.
  • Fitness: Class I medical done by selected doctors in India and list is available online.

Selection Criterion

  • There are 2 ways to join Air India either through the Cadet Pilot Program and 2nd is Applying Directly.
  • CPL (Commercial Pilot's License) Holders can directly apply.
  • In applying Directly the candidate has to pass the DGCA exams, compleete the training flying hours and get his/her Commercial Pilots License and appear for the Walk-in Interview which is followed by Type Rating.
  • In the Cadet Program everything is taken care of by the Airline from the exams to the license to the type rating and you are through.
  • Then, post the type rating the candidate is alloted the Aircraft which he/she will fly as a pilot.
  • Then you start your aviation career as a 2nd Officer and go all the way up to a Senior Commander.

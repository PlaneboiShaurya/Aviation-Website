Indian Army Aviation

About

The Indian Army Aviation boasts state-of-the-art technology helicopter fleet including Apaches, LCH, ALH Rudra and plays a crucial role in various missions and operations be it enemy strike operations and Disaster Reescue Missions. The men and women aka the Army Aviators have always served the nation.

Eligibility Conditions

  • Age: Candidates must not be older than 23 years.
  • Education: Minimum 10+2 qualification with Physics and Mathematics and must hold a graduation degree from a recognized university.
  • Nationality: Must be a citizen of India.
  • Physical Standards: As per Indian Army standards.
  • Medical Fitness: Candidates must be medically fit as per Army standards.

Selection Procedure

  • A written exam conducted by the UPSC.
  • The exam is followed by an SSB (Services Selection Board) Interview.
  • The selected candidates undergo a full-body medical check-up.
  • The merit list is prepared based on written and SSB marks.
  • Joining letters are issued based on the vacancies and merit.
